About · Product intent

pred101 exists to teach the system behind prediction markets.

This site is intentionally structured like a learning product first, not like a news feed and not like a random archive. The goal is to help users build durable understanding, execution discipline, and better research habits around prediction markets.

What pred101 is

Prediction Market 101 is a structured learning and research site built around four layers: a guided path, a strategy library, KOL research, and a report stream. Each layer has a distinct job.

What it is not

It is not trying to be a generic crypto news site, not trying to mirror every market page, and not trying to impress with volume. The standard is clarity, sequence, and usefulness.

Current structure

  • Start page for orientation and first actions
  • 7-day path for ordered learning
  • Library for durable knowledge and frameworks
  • Reports for the live update layer
  • Chinese main site for the deepest archive and freshest content
Language strategy: English provides the clean overview layer. Chinese remains the main operating layer for richer reports, deeper analysis, and denser knowledge assets.
Risk notice: pred101 is for education, research, and information organization. It is not investment advice. Prediction markets involve substantial risk and should be approached with explicit risk controls.
