🎯 Crypto Up/Down Markets

These markets predict the price direction of cryptocurrencies (BTC/ETH/SOL) at a specific point in time:

UP Shares

Worth $1.00 if price goes up

DOWN Shares

Worth $1.00 if price goes down

Settlement Time

Typically 5 min, 15 min, 1 hour

⏱️ Latency Arbitrage Principle

Price Transmission Chain

CEX Price Move (t=0ms)
    ↓
Arb Bot Detection (t=+10-15ms)
    ↓
Polymarket Price Adjustment (t=+20-35ms)
    ↓
Exploitable Window: ~5-20ms

Execution Strategy

  1. Monitor Binance/Bybit BTC/ETH prices via WebSocket
  2. Detect significant moves (>0.5%)
  3. Immediately buy corresponding direction on Polymarket
  4. Wait for market price to adjust to fair value
  5. Sell for profit or hold to settlement

⚠️ Key Risks

  • Latency trap: 2-second delay can mean instant loss on entry
  • Liquidity risk: Large orders may not exit in time
  • Gas costs: Significant percentage under high-frequency trading
  • Capital threshold: Recommend ≥$5K starting (considering gas and fees)

  • CEX API: Binance/Bybit WebSocket (free)
  • Execution speed: <500ms latency required
