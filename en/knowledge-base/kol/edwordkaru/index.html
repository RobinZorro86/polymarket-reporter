📐 Core Principle of Probabilistic Arbitrage

Polymarket differs from traditional arbitrage:

  • Cannot short or use leverage—only buy and manage exits
  • Look for probability sums below 100% (not >100% like traditional arb)
  • One YES + One NO = $1—this is the basic probability definition
