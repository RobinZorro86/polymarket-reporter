🎯 What is Pre-Market

Polymarket's Pre-Market is a special category of markets predicting future events for crypto projects. Three main types:

FDV Markets

"Will MegaETH launch day FDV exceed $2B?"

Airdrop Markets

"Will Pump.fun airdrop before March 31?"

Token Launch Markets

"Will Base launch a token before end of 2026?"

📈 Three Key Insights

1. FDV Markets are No Goldmines

  • Data: 298 settled FDV markets, 72.8% resulted in No
  • Reason: Projects are always overvalued in Pre-Market stage
  • Strategy: Focus fire on high thresholds ($4B+) buying No

2. Airdrop Markets are Nearly as Good

  • Data: 73% No win rate
  • Reason: Airdrops are always later than expected (Monad delayed from Sep to Dec)
  • Strategy: Only buy near-term expiries (30-90 days)

3. Token Launch is a Trap

  • Data: 50/50 with no alpha
  • Reason: Token launches are a matter of when, not if—long-dated markets (before end of 2026) settle Yes nearly 100%
  • Strategy: Avoid these markets
✅ Conclusion: Focus fire on FDV and Airdrop, avoid Token Launch.

🪜 Staircase Pattern

FDV markets have a particularly beautiful pattern—the staircase effect:

Principle

Take Monad as example: launch day FDV landed at $3.2B.

  • All thresholds below $3.2B ($2B, $3B) → settle Yes
  • All thresholds above $3.2B ($4B, $5B, $6B...) → settle No

Strategy Application

You don't need to predict the exact FDV—just judge "unlikely to exceed $X":

Monad case:
Buy $5B No, $6B No, $8B No, $10B No simultaneously
→ All four win

⚙️ Strategy Framework

Selection Rules

  • ✅ FDV markets: Priority
  • ✅ Airdrop markets: Optional
  • ❌ Token Launch: Avoid

Entry Rules

  • FDV: Choose mid-to-high thresholds (e.g., $3B-$4B range)
  • Airdrop: Only buy near-term expiries (30-90 days)
  • Price: No price below 70c is attractive (ROI > 43%)

Position Management

  • Single market ≤ 10% of total position
  • Same event (different thresholds) combined ≤ 30%
  • Maintain diversification with 5-10 active positions

Exit Rules

  • Core strategy: Hold to settlement (no swing trading)
  • Exception: If No price rises to 95c+ before settlement, consider selling early to lock profits

⚠️ Risks and Traps

  • Black swan events: Plasma launch FDV briefly hit $8B, all threshold Nos went to zero (27% of FDV markets result in Yes)
  • Time cost: For No @ 90c trade, winning earns only 11%—if locked for 3 months, annualized 44%
  • Liquidity risk: Only trade markets with volume >$100K
  • Token Launch trap: Markets expiring in 2026, 93% settle Yes
