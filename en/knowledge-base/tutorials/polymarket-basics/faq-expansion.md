## FAQ-02: How Do I Deposit and Withdraw?

**Category**: Account  
**Status**: ✅ Confirmed

### Short Answer
Deposit via Polygon network using USDC. Credit card purchases available.

### Deposit Methods

**1. USDC Transfer** (Recommended)
- Network: Polygon (MATIC)
- Fees: ~$0.01-0.10
- Time: Minutes

**2. Credit Card**
- Supported: Visa, Mastercard
- Fees: ~2-4%
- Time: Instant

**3. Crypto Transfer**
- Supported: ETH, MATIC, USDC
- Network: Polygon

### Withdrawal
- USDC to wallet
- Fiat via exchanges

**Source**: Polymarket Help Center

**Status**: ✅ Confirmed
