Platforms & execution

Platforms are not interchangeable. The important differences are market quality, liquidity, regulation, and execution workflow.

🎯 What to compare

  • Polymarket: rich event coverage, active flow, strong fit for research-driven and headline-driven trading.
  • Kalshi: clearer regulatory path in some categories and cleaner framing for certain event contracts.
  • In practice, compare fees, spread, depth, speed, and how well each platform fits your workflow.

🧠 Your current main battlefield

  • At this stage, Polymarket remains the primary focus because your reports, KOL system, and automation stack already revolve around it.
  • The goal is not platform hopping. The goal is to go deep on one workflow first.
Focus principle: depth beats breadth in the early stage. Master one platform before expanding.

✅ Today's task

  • Mark each category as ultra-short, intraday, or swing style.
  • Start a simple "market type → strategy type" mapping.
