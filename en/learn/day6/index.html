Day 6: Advanced Tactics & Cross-Market Thinking

Only after the base is stable should you move to more complex combinations.

Advanced directions

  • Compare pricing for the same event across multiple markets or information sets.
  • Use external news flow as event-driven trigger input.
  • Recognize emotional overreaction and forced chasing.
  • Convert KOL opinions into testable hypotheses instead of blind copying.

Common mistakes

  • Mistaking complexity for sophistication.
  • Mistaking speed for edge.
  • Using other people’s profit screenshots as your strategy framework.

Today’s task

  • Choose one event and compare at least two market or information views.
  • Write down “why the market may be wrong” instead of “why I think this goes up.”

How to use this page: finish the concept layer first, then connect it to tools, strategy pages, and daily reports so you build a full loop: concept → tools → strategy → review.
