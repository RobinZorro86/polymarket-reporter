Learning Route · 7 Days from Zero to实战

7 Days to Prediction Market Trading

30 minutes per day, from basic concepts to实战 strategies. No finance background required, just curiosity and execution.

Design Philosophy: Not "read all materials", but "complete one small task daily". After 7 days, you should be able to execute a trade independently.
Day 1 · 30 min

Prediction Market Basics

Understand what prediction markets are, how to profit, and differences from traditional investing.

  • Prediction markets vs gambling vs traditional investing
  • Binary markets, probability pricing, Yes/No contracts
  • Resolution mechanisms and settlement logic
  • Risk warning: This is NOT "easy money"
Read 15 min + Think 15 min
Day 2 · 40 min

Polymarket Platform Operations

Familiarize with Polymarket interface, market browsing, order flow.

  • Registration and account setup
  • Browse markets: Politics, Sports, Crypto, Weather
  • Understand market details: Liquidity, Volume, Expiry
  • Simulated order (no actual execution)
Read 20 min + Hands-on 20 min
Day 3 · 40 min

Wallet Setup & Security

Set up crypto wallet, fund USDC, security practices.

  • Choose wallet: MetaMask / Rabby / Others
  • Polygon network configuration
  • Buy and deposit USDC
  • Security checklist: Private keys, phishing, approval management
Setup 30 min + Security check 10 min
Day 4 · 30 min

Risk Management Principles

Learn position sizing, stop-loss, avoid common mistakes.

  • Position sizing: 2-5% of capital per trade
  • Circuit breaker: Daily max loss limit
  • Liquidity risk: Avoid small markets
  • Resolution risk: Understand "unpredictable" scenarios
Read 20 min + Create personal rules 10 min
Day 5 · 40 min

Basic Strategy: Signal Sniper

Learn first executable strategy: event-driven quick response.

  • Strategy logic: Pricing delay after news release
  • Information source prep: Twitter, News RSS, Discord
  • Execution flow: Discover → Verify → Order
  • Case study: Real trade retrospective
Learn 25 min + Simulated drill 15 min
Day 6 · 40 min

Advanced Strategy Selection

Choose deep dive based on interest: Fast-Loop / Mert Sniper / Weather Trader.

  • Fast-Loop: High-frequency cycles, BTC sprint markets
  • Mert Sniper: Near-expiry, high-certainty trades
  • Weather Trader: Alternative data, weather arbitrage
  • Selection advice: Based on your time and risk preference
Strategy learn 30 min + Decision 10 min
Day 7 · 60 min

Live Trading & KOL Research

First real trade (small amount) + learn from successful traders.

  • Execute first real trade (建议 ≤$10)
  • Document trade logic and expectations
  • Research 1-2 KOL trading frameworks
  • Create next week learning plan
Live trading 30 min + Retrospective 30 min

After Completing 7 Days, You Should:

  • ✓ Understand basic prediction market mechanics
  • ✓ Have configured wallet with small USDC balance
  • ✓ Have personal risk management rules
  • ✓ Master at least 1 executable strategy
  • ✓ Complete first real trade (profit or loss)
