Why Prediction Markets?

Prediction markets combine probability, information, and execution. Done right, they become a disciplined decision system rather than pure speculation.

📈

High Liquidity

Trade around real events with continuous pricing and active participation.

🎯

Information Aggregation

Market odds compress news, sentiment, and public expectations into one number.

💰

Arbitrage Opportunities

Find edge from mispricing, timing, overreaction, and cross-market inefficiencies.
