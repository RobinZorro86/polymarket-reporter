📈 Evaluation method

  • Importance (25%) — originality and impact of the method
  • Operability (25%) — whether normal users can actually apply it
  • Truthfulness (20%) — reliability and verifiability of claims
  • Strategy value (15%) — repeatable usefulness beyond one-off trades
  • Learning curve (10%) — cost to understand and deploy
  • Risk profile (5%) — how dangerous failure modes are
