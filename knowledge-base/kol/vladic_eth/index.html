🎯 Five trading archetypes

1. Information Arbitrage

Do not predict first—reprice faster than the market.

  • Edge source: primary information feeds + faster numerical interpretation
  • Best suited for news-native, high-speed operators

2. Market Making

Do not bet on direction—sell liquidity and earn spread plus maker incentives.

  • Edge source: inventory management, quoting discipline, volatility withdrawal logic
  • Best suited for systematic, cash-flow-oriented traders

3. Cross-Platform Arbitrage

Exploit price mismatches for the same event across venues.

  • Edge source: APIs, spread scanners, rule interpretation
  • Best suited for engineers and logic hunters

4. Delta-Neutral / Vol Trading

Treat binary contracts as option-like structures and trade the mismatch between retail probability and modeled probability.

  • Edge source: options intuition, volatility modeling, derivatives execution
  • Best suited for quants and options traders

5. Whale Tracking

Do not search for alpha directly—search for people who probably have it.

  • Edge source: wallet tracking, PnL filtering, style consistency
  • Main risk: you are always one step behind the whale

✅ How to use this page
